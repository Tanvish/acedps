Mock Stock

"Subject To Market Risk."

Participants: 2

Subject to Market Risk is a financial market simulation comprising of investment in Companies, Currencies and Commodities.
Participants will be allotted a fixed amount of capital in the beginning of each round.
The first two rounds will focus on investment in Companies, Currencies and Commodities on the basis of news and rumours. A set of company Balance Sheets will be provided along with financial ratios and statements in the final round based on which the participants will invest in the respective companies.

*Teams may bring their own calculators; the school will not provide any.*
*Using phones/electronic devices during the competition is strictly prohibited.*

Vivaad

“Don't raise your voice, improve your argument."- Desmond Tutu

Eligibility: XI-XII

Tryst With Economy

"If its doesnt sell it isnt creative"

Participants: 5

Eligibility: Open

Tryst with Economy is an economic simulation where participants will be provided with an economic problem and they would have to design a policy, program or a set of actions to solve the same. The economic problem would be ambiguous in nature and can be related to the past, present or the future, to India, European Union, or any other country or group of countries in the world.
The participants have to present their policy or programme, on the day of the Competition, 3 November 2017. The participants have to give an oral presentation and also submit a hard copy of their policy or program.
The policy or program made by the participants should include a statistical and economic study of the problem, the drafting, and implementation of the policy or program and any other aspects the participants may wish to add.

The Economic Adversity will be emailed to the Student Representative of the school on 20th of October.
The participants may refer to, but not plagiarize existing policies and programs. Plagiarism will lead to disqualification; any references must be cited in the hard copy.
Each participant will be allotted 5 minutes for their presentation.
There is no word limit for the hard copy.

Judging Criteria: Overall policy, program and related criterions; creativity, critical thinking, economic acumen and analysis.

For Further Details Contact:

Vedant Madan: +9185278161783
